The Heart

The parasympathetic division acts on the MAChRs of the heart to decrease the heart rate, and contractility.

The sympathetic division acts on the β2 adrenergic receptors of the heart to increase the heart rate and contractility. Try clicking the buttons to toggle the effects of each division.
